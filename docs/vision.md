# Vision

Riftbound is a tactical trading card game with high readability and sharp tactical depth. The digital client should:

- Make complex states **legible** at a glance.
- Reward mastery without overwhelming newcomers.
- Support rapid iteration on card balance and new sets.

## Pillars
1. **Clarity** — Every action is visible and replayable.
2. **Tension** — Turns feel punchy, and momentum is always in flux.
3. **Expression** — Decks and cosmetics tell a player story.
